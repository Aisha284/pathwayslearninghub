Who We Are

More than tutoring — we're a community.

Pathways Learning Hub is a nonprofit dedicated to providing high-quality tutoring and enrichment for youth in Kent, Washington. Our mission is to support academic success through personalized guidance, engaging activities, and a safe, encouraging environment.

Through sports, team-building, book clubs, college readiness, and unforgettable community events — we help every child reach their full potential and every family feel at home.

Our Mission
"To support academic success and social growth by offering personalized guidance, enriching activities, and a safe environment where every child can reach their full potential."

Our Story

Founded in Kent, rooted in community.

Pathways Learning Hub was established in 2024 by Somali women in Kent, Washington who saw a need — and decided to meet it. What started as afterschool tutoring has grown into a full ecosystem of programs serving youth and families.

We are proudly Somali-founded, women-led, and committed to making every program 100% free and accessible to all.
